Streaks

A streak counts consecutive days a habit was done. With forgiveness on, a small number of missed days within any 7-day window won't reset the streak. Streak logic itself is implemented in Task 8 — this setting only stores the preference.

Pillar score thresholds

Each pillar's score combines the global signals below with active habits. Leave a field blank to disable that signal (it won't add a slot to the pillar score).

Sleep

Sleep hours in this range count as one met signal for the Sleep pillar.

Sport

Steps at the selected bucket or higher count as one met signal for the Sport pillar.

Food

Water at the selected level or higher counts as one signal; meals at or above the threshold count as another.

About

health_app — local gamified health tracker for one user, running on macOS. Four pillars (sleep / sport / food + experimental "other"), daily check-in, dashboard with score, trends, and bonus stars.
