Streaks

A streak counts consecutive days a habit was done. With forgiveness on, a small number of missed days within any 7-day window won't reset the streak. Streak logic itself is implemented in Task 8 — this setting only stores the preference.

Pillar score thresholds (sleep range, steps / water buckets, meals minimum) live on the Habits tab since they describe how the things you track get scored.

About

health_app — local gamified health tracker for one user, running on macOS. Four pillars (sleep / sport / food + experimental "other"), daily check-in, dashboard with score, trends, and bonus stars.
